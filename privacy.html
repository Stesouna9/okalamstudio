Privacy Policy

VoteDay
Last updated: March 23, 2026
🛡️Your data, your control

OKALAM Studio develops and publishes VoteDay. We believe in full transparency about data collection. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how your information stays protected.

📋Data collected

VoteDay collects the following data, solely for the app to function:

  • User identifier — A unique ID generated locally to identify your account.
  • Display name & avatar — The nickname and emoji or photo you choose for your profile.
  • Votes — Your answers to daily questions.
  • Chat messages — Messages exchanged with your friends in the app.
  • Friend code — A numeric identifier that lets other users add you.
⚙️How we use your data
  • App functionality: votes, results, friends, chat
  • Cross-device sync via iCloud
  • Displaying aggregated and anonymous results
☁️Storage & security

All data is stored via Apple iCloud (CloudKit) and is subject to Apple's privacy policy. Data is protected by Apple's standard encryption (TLS/HTTPS).

🚫No data sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. No data is used for advertising or tracking purposes.

👁️Tracking & advertising

VoteDay contains no trackers, no advertising SDKs, and does not track your activity outside the app.

✅Your rights
  • Delete your account and data by uninstalling the app
  • Request data deletion by contacting us
